# Privacy Policy
*Effective Date: Today*

1. **What data is collected?**
RAKSHAK collects your location (for accurate geospatial dispatching), microphone readings (on-device only for distress keywords), and your trusted emergency contacts list.

2. **How is data stored?**
All user profiles and metadata reside securely inside a MongoDB database isolated through strict read/write protocols. Media generated (Audio or Video evidence) during emergencies are strictly AES-256 encrypted. 

3. **Who can access your emergency reports?**
Your exact physical location during a triggered crisis is ONLY relayed to your predetermined set of **Trusted Contacts**, pushed forcefully via our notification pipelines. The general public only receives broad, fully-anonymous pings that someone "Nearby" needs assistance.

4. **Data Deletion**
You own your data. End users can irrevocably erase their historical evidence footprint utilizing our deletion systems (ex. `DELETE /api/evidence/<id>`).

5. **Third-Parties**
RAKSHAK does not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your Personally Identifiable Information to outside parties.
